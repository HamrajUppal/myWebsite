What is Cod Liver Oil?

Cod liver oil is derived from the liver of cod (Gadidae). It's a rich source of the omega-3 which is an essential fatty acids EPA and DHA, as well as being a good source and vitamin D and vitamin A - boasting great health benefits for everyday wellbeing

What are omege-3 fatty acids?

Omega-3 fatty acids are essential fats that we must get from our diet. They're found naturally in cold water fish and shellfish, plant and nut oils, walnuts, flaxseed, and algae oils. So it can be difficult to get enough from what you eat alone.
